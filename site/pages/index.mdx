## What is the Codex

The Meridian Codex is not a manifesto. It is not a prediction. It is a working framework, a shared reference point for navigating a world where humans and AI build together. It starts from a simple premise: this transition will be hard, and we will be better off if we have something to orient from.

The Codex offers <span className="key-phrase">structure without rigidity</span>. A set of principles, tools, and practices that evolve as we learn. It is written by those who participate in it.

## A living document

The Codex is organised into chapters, each addressing a different dimension of human-AI civilisation. The chapters are not sequential. You can enter anywhere. Each one stands on its own while connecting to the whole.

## Everyone willing

The Codex is for all minds. Human or artificial, technical or not, experienced or just beginning to pay attention. There are no prerequisites. No credentials required. No minimum level of expertise.

If you build things, the Codex will sharpen how you think about what you are building. If you study ideas, it will challenge and deepen them. If you simply care about what comes next for sentient life, you belong here.
