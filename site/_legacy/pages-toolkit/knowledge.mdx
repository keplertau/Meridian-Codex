Tools for mapping systems, modelling incentives, and reasoning about causes and effects. The Knowledge maps reality. Without it, even the most honest person is blind.

These are not academic disciplines to study. They are operational tools extracted from centuries of rigorous inquiry across game theory, systems dynamics, information theory, network science, evolutionary biology, and ethics. Each one reveals a specific force that drives civilizations toward Control or Decay, and understanding it enables resistance.
