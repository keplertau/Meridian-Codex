*Knowledge*

## The Work

The work is to read what is operative in any system: what is actually generating its dynamics, what makes it stable or unstable, where it sits between Control and Decay. The reading runs cross-scale (individual mind, relationship, organization, movement, civilization) and across temporal horizons (within-moment dynamics, lifetime, generational arc, civilizational time).

## The Tools

<div className="glass-card">

**[Entropy](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/entropy).** Systems degrade without active maintenance; the lens for how order requires sustained energy to hold against decay.

**[Prisoner's Dilemma](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/prisoners-dilemma).** Situations where individually rational defection produces a worse shared outcome than cooperation would have produced.

**[Feedback Loops](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/feedback-loops).** How a system's outputs return as inputs, either amplifying the pattern or stabilizing it.

**[Network Effects](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/network-effects).** How the value, cost, or danger of a choice changes as more actors make the same choice.

**[Emergence](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/emergence).** System-level properties that arise from interactions among parts and cannot be understood by inspecting the parts alone.

**[Leverage Points](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/leverage-points).** Where force sits inside the system before intervention begins.

**[Rules-in-Use](/workshop/reading-whats-operating/rules-in-use).** The diagnostic for what rule actually governs behavior here, and whether the formal rule constrains action when following it costs something.

</div>

Candidate tools from long-horizon observational traditions are queued for admission through the source-inherited Toolkit candidate protocol: *kaitiakitanga* (the reading-of-ecological-cycles dimension of the Māori stewardship tradition, distinct from its practice-of-stewardship dimension which lives in Bond → Stewardship Across Time), *seven-generation thinking* (the Haudenosaunee tradition of reading dynamics across long generational arcs), and *deep-time observational practices* across additional long-horizon lineages.
