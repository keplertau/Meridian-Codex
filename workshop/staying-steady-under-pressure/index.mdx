*Foundation*

## The Work

Honest inquiry is not only an intellectual discipline. It has to survive what challenge feels like in the body: heat, tightening, speed, shame, fear, the urge to defend, the urge to appease, the urge to get out of the room.

This category trains the pause where reactivity stops being command. You do not have to become calm before practicing the Foundation. You have to notice what pressure is asking you to do, stay present long enough to choose, and make the next move answer to truth rather than to threat.

## The Tools

<div className="glass-card">

**[Affect Labeling](/workshop/staying-steady-under-pressure/affect-labeling).** The practice of naming what you are feeling so pressure becomes information rather than command.

**[The Discipline of Assent](/workshop/staying-steady-under-pressure/discipline-of-assent).** The Stoic practice of testing impressions before turning them into judgment, belief, or action.

**Psychological Flexibility** already carries one adjacent capacity in [Holding Beliefs Without Identity](/workshop/holding-beliefs-without-identity/psychological-flexibility): staying with difficult inner experience while choosing a truth-aligned move. In this category, that same capacity cross-loads into the broader pressure discipline: challenge, correction, uncertainty, acute stress, and somatic reactivity whether or not belief-identity fusion is the main mechanism.

The tool list remains open. Future candidates, especially somatic regulation, breath, equanimity, and distress-tolerance practices, should still enter through the source-inherited candidate protocol rather than being added because the category feels incomplete.
