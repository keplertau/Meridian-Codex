## The Work

Seeing is not the end of Knowledge. If perception never changes action, it becomes spectatorship with better vocabulary.

This category holds the moment after a person, team, or institution has read something real about the world: a pattern, constraint, harm, incentive, opening, or failure. The question is what action can be taken that remains faithful to what was seen. That rules out three evasions: forcing the world to match a preferred model, calling delay humility, or using analysis as shelter from responsibility. The action has to follow the grain of reality as it has been read.

<span className="key-phrase">Acting on What You See is the discipline of letting perception become movement without turning movement into force.</span>

## The Tools

<div className="glass-card">

**[Wu Wei](/workshop/acting-on-what-you-see/wu-wei).** The source-inherited practice of non-forcing action: intervening from contact with conditions rather than from domination, impatience, or self-display. Ziran is carried inside this profile as the naturalness or self-so ordering that wu wei answers to, not as a separate v0.1 tool.

**[Satyagraha](/workshop/acting-on-what-you-see/satyagraha).** The source-inherited practice of truth-force: acting publicly from fidelity to truth through nonviolent discipline, willingness to bear cost, and refusal to coerce the opponent into compliance.
