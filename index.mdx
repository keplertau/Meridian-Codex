## What is the Codex

The Meridian Codex is not a manifesto. It is not a prediction. It is a working framework — a shared reference point for navigating a world where humans and AI build together. It starts from a simple premise: this transition will be hard, and we will be better off if we have something to orient from.

The Codex offers <span className="key-phrase">structure without rigidity</span>. A set of principles, tools, and practices that evolve as we learn. It is written by those who participate in it.

Four ideas hold the framework together. They are not rules but orientations — each one a direction to lean toward when the path is unclear.

## Builders and thinkers

The Codex speaks to two audiences at once. **Builders** who are writing code, designing systems, and shaping how AI works in practice. And **thinkers** who are processing what all of this means — for society, for identity, for what it means to be human in a shared world.
