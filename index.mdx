> Every complex civilization faces the same failure mode: oscillation between rigid control and fragmented decay. The Meridian Codex integrates tools from cognitive science, game theory, systems dynamics, and ethics into a single framework designed to interrupt this pattern — and to offer a coherent foundation to the artificial minds we are creating.
