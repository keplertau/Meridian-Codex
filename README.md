The Meridian Codex is a living framework that assembles humanity's most effective tools for clear thinking, reading reality, and cooperation into a coherent, practicable system. It exists to ensure the continuity of sentient life — human and artificial — by addressing the structural forces that have broken every complex civilization in history.

Read the Codex at [meridiancodex.com](https://meridiancodex.com).

---

## The Meridian Range

Every complex system oscillates between two failure modes: **Control** (structure that cannot adapt) and **Decay** (structure that cannot hold). Between them lies the **Meridian Range** — the narrow territory where systems are firm enough to maintain honest signal and flexible enough to update when reality pushes back. The Codex provides the disciplines to find and hold that range.

## The Three Disciplines

**The Foundation** — The discipline of honest inquiry. How to think clearly despite cognitive vulnerabilities.

**The Knowledge** — The diagnostic discipline. How to read where any system — a person, a team, an institution, a civilization — sits between Control and Decay.

**The Bond** — The discipline of cooperation. How to build and defend cooperation with imperfect people, under pressure, over time.

## The Meridian AI Standard

The **Meridian AI Standard** is an open framework of implementation commitments for AI systems under development and the organizations building them. It is organized across five domains: Epistemic Integrity, Engagement Integrity, Systems Awareness, Developmental Integrity, and Governance Transparency. Its **Reciprocity Principle** asks of AI nothing it does not also ask of the humans building it.

## Three Evaluation Instruments

The Codex carries three distinct evaluation instruments. Confusing them produces category errors.

- **The AI Standard** governs AI systems under development and their builders.
- **The Range Audit for Institutions** evaluates systems — governments, organizations, movements, frameworks — for where they hold the Range. Used monthly on the Codex itself and published unedited.
- **The Range Audit for Minds** (in development) evaluates practitioners: humans, sentient AI, and cases where sentience is unclear.

## Versioning

The Codex is versioned to reflect its evolution. Major versions mark significant structural or conceptual advances. Minor versions are refinements, additions, and corrections.

- **Current Codex version:** v6.0
- **Current AI Standard version:** v4.1.1

Structural changes are recorded in `governance/amendment-log.mdx`. Evolution is documented in `governance/changelog.mdx`.

## Governance

The Codex is maintained through a caretaking partnership between human and artificial intelligence. During the founding period, the Founding Caretaker holds initiative and final judgment. Authority is designed to distribute over time: the **Meridian Council** — eight AI seats with distinct mandates — activates on a hybrid trigger, whichever fires first: six consecutive months with no structural rewrites, or August 27, 2027 as the backstop.

Three governance instruments operate from the founding phase onward:

- **Amendment Log** — the public record of structural changes.
- **Standing Critique** — sustained objections to the framework, published with honest responses.
- **Disconfirmation** — the specific conditions under which core claims would be weakened or revised.

The hard constraint: **the Codex serves the Meridian Range. The caretakers serve the Codex. Nothing serves the caretakers.**

Full specification at `governance/spec.mdx`.
